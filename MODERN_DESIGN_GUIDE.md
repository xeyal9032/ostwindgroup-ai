### 🎨 **Tasarım Detayları:**

#### **Input Container:**
```css
- Background: backdrop-blur-sm
- Border: border-border/50
- Shadow: shadow-lg hover:shadow-xl
- Border radius: rounded-2xl
- Focus ring: ring-primary/20
```

#### **Action Buttons:**
```css
- Size: h-7 w-7
- Shape: rounded-full
- Hover: bg-primary/10
- Transition: duration-200
```

#### **Send Button:**
```css
- Shape: rounded-full
- Shadow: shadow-lg hover:shadow-xl
- Animation: transition-all duration-200
```
